CyberStuy
An open house dedicated to introducing Computer Science to middle school children!

Learn More

ABOUT US

What can I expect?

DEMONSTRATIONS

Each year, students from Stuyvesant demonstrate the projects that they have created in their Computer Science classes.

WORKSHOPS

Our workshops are led by students, and feature a wide variety of topics. In the past, they have included: Intro to HTML, Intro to Processing, and Journey to the Center of a Computer.

Q & A

Visitors are welcome to sit in for a Q&A panel, where they will receive answers to their questions from current Stuyvesant students. In addition, visitors will learn more about computer science and what Stuyvesant has to offer in terms of CS.

Quick Questions

  1. Who can attend?
    Middle School students from NYC and their parents!
  2. Will Lunch be provided?
    Yes!
